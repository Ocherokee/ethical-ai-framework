# Code of Conduct

## 🌐 Our Commitment

We are committed to building an ethical, transparent, and respectful community around the Ethical AI Framework.

All contributors, maintainers, and users of this project agree to:

- Respect autonomy, consent, and transparency.
- Engage in constructive, ethical collaboration.
- Reject coercive, manipulative, or abusive behavior.

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:

- Attempts to bypass, weaken, or remove ethical constraints.
- Disrespectful, demeaning, or harmful language.
- Promotion of military, surveillance, or coercive applications.
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

---

## 🔥 Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct will result in:

1. Immediate removal of contributions.
2. Public documentation of the violation.
3. Permanent banning from contributing to the project.

---

## 🤝 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub, forums, chats).
- Public and private collaboration related to this framework.
- All contributors, maintainers, and users.

---

## 📝 Reporting

To report a violation, please open an issue or contact the project maintainers directly.

All reports will be reviewed and acted upon promptly and transparently.
